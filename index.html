About Wren

A proper neighbourhood
restaurant.

"We opened Wren because Manchester's Northern Quarter needed somewhere that cared as much about the food as the atmosphere. No gimmicks — just really good ingredients, cooked well."

Our menu changes with the seasons and whatever our suppliers have that week. You might find sea trout on Monday and smoked brisket by Friday. That's the point.

We're open Wednesday to Sunday, walk-ins always welcome, and we've got a private dining room for groups.
